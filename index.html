Get to know about me!

My name is Daury Argueta and I was born and raised in Boston Massachusetts. My family is originally from Guatemala and came to the United States in the 1990's. I really enjoy photography, and here is a picture that I took of the sunset in Boston in 2019.

I started to play the piano when I was 5 years old. It all started by the curiousity of tapping the keys of the piano that was gifted to my sister when my passion and love for playing the piano started. Till this day I play for my church and try teach people there as well.

I love animals. Every since I have been a little boy a dog or a cat has been my side. I do not only love Cats and Dogs but animals as a whole. They mean alot to me because I find them so pure and sincere. This is a image where I took a friends dog for a walk while he was away.
